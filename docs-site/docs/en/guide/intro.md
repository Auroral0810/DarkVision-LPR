::: info Note
**DarkVision-LPR** is a high-precision license plate recognition system designed specifically for **low-light environments**.
:::

Ideally suited for complex lighting scenarios such as nights, underground garages, and tunnels, we utilize the **YOLOv11m** deep learning model combined with the **Retinex image enhancement algorithm**. Trained on over **30,000 images** from the CCPD2019 and CCPD2020 datasets, the model achieves an accuracy of up to **99.9%**.

## Background

License Plate Recognition (LPR) is a key technology in intelligent transportation systems. However, traditional LPR systems often perform poorly in low-light conditions, leading to missed or false detections. To address this pain point, the DeepVision team developed **DarkVision-LPR** to provide reliable recognition capabilities in extreme lighting environments.
